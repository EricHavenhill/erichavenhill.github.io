I am an Instructor in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at [Florida Polytechnic University](https://floridapoly.edu/), where I teach and conduct research at the intersection of dynamics, control, and computational methods.

My research interests include classical, optimal, and nonlinear control, robotic systems, biomechanics, and numerical methods. I am broadly interested in developing principled approaches to modeling and controlling dynamic systems, with applications spanning robotics and cellular movement.

## Teaching

I teach courses in mechanical engineering and general engineering. I have taught the following courses:
- EGN 1213 - Engineering Computation: MATLAB
- EGN 3321 - Dynamics
- MECH 307 - Mechatronics
- MECH 342 - Fluid Mechanics
- MECH 417 - Control Systems

## Research Interests

- Robotics and autonomous systems
- Classical, optimal, and nonlinear control
- Dynamic systems and computational mechanics
- Biomechanics and cellular locomotion
- Numerical methods
